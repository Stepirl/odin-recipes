Simple Buttercake

Ingredients

  • 125g Butter/Margerine
  • 3/4 cup Sugar
  • 1 tsp Vanilla Essence
  • 2 Eggs
  • 2 cups Self Raising Flour
  • 1/2 cup Milk
  • pinch of Salt

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 160°c fan forced (180°c Conventional)
  2. Grease and line cake tray
  3. Mix butter and Sugar together
  4. Add vanilla essence to the butter and sugar mix
  5. Add eggs 1 at a time while mixing the butter, sugar and vanilla essence together
  6. Sift flour and salt into the mixture and stir
  7. Add half a cup of milk and mix thoroughly
  8. Pour mixture into cake tray
  9. Put cake tray into the over for 20-25 minutes
  10. Let it rest for 5 minutes after taking out of the oven
Enjoy - My mum
