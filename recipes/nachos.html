Nachos.

This dish is relatively simple to make and tastes incredibly good with very little effort and not all of the ingredients are necessary but they do certainly help it taste much MUCH better. Feel free to add more or less of certain ingredients so that the food can fit your taste much better.

Ingredients

  • 3 Tomatoes
  • 1/2 Onion
  • 1 Jalapeño
  • 2tsp Lime juice
  • 1/2 Chopped Coriander/Cilantro
  • A pinch of Salt
  • A pinch of Pepper
  • A pinch of Dried Oregano
  • A pinch of Ground Cumin
  • 500g Corn chips
  • 2 cups Grated Cheese

Steps

  1. Dice the tomatoes, onion, jalapeño and coriander/cilantro into small pieces and put it in a bowl with the lime juice and mix thoroughly until satisfied.
  2. Sprinkle the salt, pepper, oregano and ground cumin in the bowl with the tomatoes, onion, jalapeño and coriander/cilantro. Mix again until satisfied.
  3. Preheat oven to 180°c fan forced (200°c fonventional)
  4. Spread your corn chips on a tray then lightly sprinkle 1/2 cup grated cheese over them.
  5. Spread the salsa evenly over all of the corn chips.
  6. Using the rest of the grated cheese, cover the corn chips as evenly as possible.
  7. Place the tray with the corn chips in the over for roughly five minutes (or until the cheese browns and melts)

Enjoy your Nachos!
